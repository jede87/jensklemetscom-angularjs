lifehacks that have helped me

1. make your stomach happy

every morning before work, add 1 tbsp of whole flaxseed to a glass of water. drink the flaxseed water the same evening. your digestion system will thank you.

2. intermitten fasting

start with skipping breakfast and keep on going until lunch time and eat a big lunch. eat a snack a few hours later. eat a big dinner and try to not eating anything after that. aim for a 12 hour window at first.

read the leangains guide to get your intermitten fasting going

3. work out

do not spend more than 1 hour training at the gym

example day

  • 1. warmup 2 - 10 minutes on a rowing machine, cross trainer, running mat or exersice bike
  • 2. main lift 3 sets of 5 reps. on last set do as many reps as possible. add weight next time doing the exercise if you could do more than 5 on last set.
  • 3. assistance work. choose 2 exercises and do 3 sets of 10 reps for each excersice.
  • 4. go home

a good starting point: Jim Wendler: 5/3/1 for a beginner

avoid fuckarounditis: Martin Berkhan: fuckarounditis

a guide to reverse pyramid training: Martin Berkhan: reverse pyramid training guide

4. guided meditation

open up your favorite search enginge and do a search for "guided meditation". choose one that is around 15 - 30 minute in the begining and find what works for you. i prefer listening to yoga nidra.
